Base shapes:

Coccus - spherical
Bacillus - rod-shaped
Spirillum - big spirals
Spirochetes - small spirals
Vibrio - comma-shaped


Modifiers:

Strepto: a strip
    - Example: streptococci (a strip of spherical bacteria)

Diplo: two
    - Example: diplobacilli (two rod-shaped bacteria)

Staphlo: a grape-like bunch
    - Example: staphylococci (grape-like bunch of bacteria)
